Step 2: Add to Merkle Tree ?
Merkle Tree Insertion
Your commitment becomes a leaf in the tree. The tree is append-only (no deletions) to maintain integrity.

Key Points:
- Position is public but commitment is private
- Tree root changes with each insertion
- Old roots remain valid for proving

Step 3: Generate Merkle Proof ?
Merkle Path Generation
Creates a proof of membership without revealing which leaf is yours.

Path Elements: Sibling hashes at each level
Path Indices: Whether you're left (0) or right (1) child

With these, anyone can verify your leaf reaches the root!

Step 4: Generate ZK Proof ?
Groth16 Proof Generation
Creates a succinct proof (only 192 bytes!) that proves:
- You know a valid commitment in the tree
- You know its secret preimage
- You haven't withdrawn it before

All without revealing which commitment!
